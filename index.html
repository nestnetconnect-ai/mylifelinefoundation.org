Who We Are

About My Lifeline
Foundation

My Lifeline Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities in rural Odisha. We focus on education, health awareness, and youth development to create sustainable change and improve quality of life.

Incorporated under Section 8 of the Companies Act 2013, we operate with full transparency and community trust.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower rural communities through education, health awareness, and youth development programs that create long-term positive impact.

🌅 Our Vision

A society where every child has access to education, every family has basic health awareness, and every youth has opportunity and guidance.

🎓 Education Program

Our Programs

Education Support Initiative

MY LIFELINE FOUNDATION is determined to fulfill the dreams of rural students of Odisha and providing them with right career guidance and study materials for higher education.

Education is both the means as well as the end to a better life — it empowers an individual to earn his/her livelihood and increases one's awareness on healthcare, social behavior, rights, and becoming a better citizen.

🏫 MLF School Initiative

MY LIFELINE FOUNDATION School — Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Centre running for underprivileged children.

Home / Programs / Health

🩺 Health Awareness Program

Health & Wellness

Health Awareness & Care

My Lifeline Foundation seeks to ensure the survival, protection and healthy development of children and the reproductive health of adolescents and adults, especially women of child bearing age.

Two-pronged approach — bring quality healthcare services to doorsteps of the needy, and promote healthcare awareness and contemporary healthcare seeking behavior among the underprivileged.

🧘 Featured Activities

MLF Yoga Centre · Blood Donation Camp · SHG Formation and Women Empowerment

Home / Programs / Rural Youth Development

🌾 Rural Youth Development

Livelihood & Skills

Rural Youth Development Program

My Lifeline Foundation seeks to increase food security and family disposable income which will enable families to improve their children's welfare.

This national level programme trains the poor and underprivileged youth in English Proficiency, Basic Computer Education and Soft Skills for employment in the fast expanding retail, hospitality and BPO sectors.

🌱 Featured Activities

SHG Formation · Demo Farm · Kitchen Garden · Women Empowerment Programs

🌿 Environment Program

Planet & People

Environmental Awareness & Action

India faces millions of deaths each year due to the adverse effects of our day-to-day activities on the environment. My Lifeline Foundation works to raise awareness and bring ownership within people to save the environment.

🌱 NIRA — Nature Is Requesting Action

Through NIRA, we work to combat environment degradation, promote sustainability, and help communities with greener, safer, healthier environments across India.

🏫 Green Your School

Annual program for schools across India — shortlisted schools receive cash awards for implementing green building initiatives.

Activities

Environment Activities

🌳

Plantation Drive

Plantation at schools, colleges and public places to increase green cover in Odisha.

🧹

Cleanliness Drive

Village and public place cleanliness campaigns — making communities cleaner and healthier.

⛺

Work Camp

Community work camps to address local environmental issues through collective action.
