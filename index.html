About Us

Building Brighter Futures
Across Odisha

Founded in 2018, My Lifeline Foundation is a youth-led NGO committed to creating lasting change at the grassroots level. We believe every life holds immeasurable potential — and we work tirelessly to unlock it.

🔭
Our Vision
A self-reliant Odisha where every individual has equal access to education, health, and opportunity.
🎯
Our Mission
To mobilize youth power and community resources to eradicate poverty and inequality across all 30 districts.
🤝
Compassionate Action
We lead with empathy — understanding community needs before designing solutions that truly work.
🌱
Sustainable Growth
Every program is built to empower communities to sustain themselves long after we leave.
🔍
Radical Transparency
We maintain open books and publish annual impact reports so every rupee is accounted for.
